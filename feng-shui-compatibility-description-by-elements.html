Feng Shui People Compatibility

Description of compatibility by elements

    To display a description of compatibility by elements, you must execute the command of the same name, which is located in the "≡" menu.

    In this window, a description of the compatibility of the two partners is formed according to their pillars. At the top of the window is the compatibility indicator, which gives a brief assessment of the compatibility for this method. The following colors are used to indicate the degree of compatibility:
green - good compatibility;
gray - medium compatibility;
red - bad compatibility.
    The program calculates potential relationships between partners, which are inherent in them by nature (as Feng Shui says). However, in life, the nature of relationships can be influenced by other factors that are not considered in the program. For a more complete introduction, you can read the description. If desired, you can get acquainted with the characteristics of each partner.
    Experts can do more detailed analysis using the Pillars and Compatibility by Elements windows.
Below is a more detailed description of compatibility by elements.

    Descriptions generated by the application can be saved to files using the penultimate button on the toolbar. The files obtained in this way can be sent to friends and acquaintances (see Demonstration of settings and work with the application "People's Feng Shui Compatibility" for Android).
    By clicking the last button on the toolbar, the description can be slightly corrected (if so desired). The description must be edited before saving it to a file.

    All descriptions are saved in the application folder and are available using the "Saved descriptions" command.

ASTROLOGICAL COMPATIBILITY BY ANIMAL SIGN OF THE YEAR

    Rooster and monkey

    Although there is no significant disharmony in the combination of your signs, you have differences that deserve attention. You are both alert, resourceful and have the ability to achieve your goals. However, the pedantic Rooster can annoy the Monkey with its attention to details, and the restless Monkey can lead the Rooster into confusion with its unexpected decisions and bold antics. A rooster who loves consistency in everything can harshly criticize the Monkey. On the other hand, the Monkey can lose patience and offend the Rooster. Both of you are straightforward and rather self-centered. Nevertheless, if you work on yourself and bring your differences closer, you will enjoy the common features of your character. Both of you are warmhearted, love to attract attention and need praise. Therefore, if you pay attention to each other and praise each other for those wonderful qualities that you undoubtedly possess, you can make each other very happy.

COMPATIBILITY BY ANIMAL SIGN OF THE YEAR

    ROOSTER AND MONKEY. Both of you like a beautiful life, you and two are interested, but the Rooster is too straightforward, while the Monkey is cunning. The monkey often hides his plans, which confuses the Rooster, who prefers to know exactly what is happening, and who has established views. The rooster is prone to immediate action and is not afraid to speak openly, while the shrewd Monkey likes to evaluate and analyze the situation in order to circumvent difficulties with the least losses. Both of you need independence, so it’s best to give each other a certain freedom in relationships and be tolerant, and then your opposite traits will complement each other perfectly.

ASTROLOGICAL COMPATIBILITY BY ANIMAL SIGN OF THE DAY

    Dragon and Monkey

    This is an excellent combination. However, since both of you are prone to selfishness and aggressiveness, you need to avoid certain pitfalls if you want your relationship to last long enough. Both partners have dictatorial habits, so it is necessary to separate the spheres of influence. Ideally, you could rule the world together. But if you disagree strongly, skirmishes may flare up between you, leading to grave consequences if you do not learn to find a middle ground. The least that can be said about your relationship is that you have a lot to learn about each other. And if you learn to extinguish the flames of discord that break out in time, love will turn your life into a real holiday.

COMPATIBILITY BY ANIMAL SIGN OF THE DAY

    DRAGON AND MONKEY. Both are capable of much, their connection can significantly exceed the romantic relationships of other characters. The dragon is smart, strong, attractive, but this does not bother the Monkey, because she knows how to charm and seduce. Usually they literally bombard each other with ideas and plans, so to a large extent this is an inventive and creative union. The dragon, more than the Monkeys, needs to be admired, he likes recognition and praise, and the cunning Monkey knows how to do it. A monkey can be enchanted, but it cannot be blinded by passion or fooled. These two characters know exactly how to captivate each other, and can make a perfect pair.

COMPATIBILITY BY HEAVENLY ELEMENTS OF THE YEAR

    Water and Water

    If the element of the day or year of birth for you and your partner is Water, you will be in complete harmony with each other, provided that one of you has Water yin and the other has Water yang. If both of you have only Water Yin or Water Yang, you will encounter each other from time to time.
