Feng Shui calculator

Application settings

    To customize the display of the required information and perform calculations in accordance with the method used, use the right menu on the "≡" toolbar.
    All commands, except for Settings (Настройки), are used to quickly switch to the desired type of displayed information (the first two groups of commands) and the desired calculation option (the last two groups of commands) in the application.

    Display of the elements
    This group includes the following commands:
    Names of the elements - displays the names of the elements of the elements with an indication of belonging to yin (a name with a small letter) or yang (a name with a capital letter) and the names of animals in the earthly elements (a name with a small letter is yin, a name with a capital letter is yang). The merged elements are displayed in large letters;
    Aspects of the elements - displays aspects of the elements (Personality, Intelligence, Wealth, Power, Resources) indicating the value of the calculated auspiciousness for each element;
    Hieroglyphs - the names of the elements are displayed in the form of hieroglyphs.

    Display of hidden heavenly stems
    This group of commands sets the information that will be displayed in the bottom element of each pillar:
    Hidden heavenly stems - heavenly stems hidden in earthly branches. Elemental names are displayed as the initial letter of its name. If the letter is large - the yang element, the small letter is the yin element. For example: t+w+E means yin tree, yin water and yang Earth;
    Dominant hidden heavenly stems - displays the elements in the hidden heavenly stems that have the greatest impact;
    Qi phases 1st degree - the relation of the daytime dominate to the earthly branch: embryo, feeding, birth, bathing in favors, putting on a belt, joining the service, peak of prosperity, withering, disease, death, storage, break;
    Qi phases 2nd degree - the relation of the heavenly trunk to the earthly branch on which it "sits": embryo, nutrition, growth and development, dirty water, hat and belt, composing happiness, imperial lamps, weakening, illness, death, grave, disappearance of decomposition;
    Qi phases 3rd degree - the relation of the dominant hidden celestial trunk to the earthly branch in which it is hidden: neutral phases of Chi, luminous phases of Chi, dying phases of Chi;
    60-year cycle number - is the ordinal number of the year in the 60-year cycle of the Chinese calendar.

    Merging elements
    This group of commands manages the accounting of the merging of the elements. It includes three commands:
    No merge - mergers of the elements will not be taken into account in the calculations and, accordingly, the elements will not change, but mergers will be displayed above and below the pillars of fate, the calculated date and in the section Merging and collisions of the pillars of Fate with the pillars of the calculated date;
    Within Date - Mergers will be counted within the date of birth and calculated date. The merging of the elements between the pillars of the two dates will not be counted;
    Transit - merges will be counted within the date of birth, calculated date and between the pillars of the two dates.

    Hour duration
    Constant - every Chinese hour will correspond to two astronomical hours;
    Variable - the duration of the Chinese hour will depend on the geographical latitude of the place that is indicated as the place of birth of the person and the place of calculation (place of stay of the person). 


Command Settings (Настройки)

    This command displays a dialog containing three tabs. On the GENERAL tab you can configure: 

    Palaces of life - how the Palaces of life will be located:
    Traditional - the following sequence of Palaces is used: Life Fate, Brothers sisters, Relationships,
Children grandchildren, Power wealth, Place living, Employees salary, Servants slaves, Diseases dangers, Ethics religion, Movement, Parents;
    New - a different sequence of Palaces is used: Life Fate, Power wealth, Brothers Sisters, Parents, Children grandchildren, Servants slaves, Relationships, Diseases dangers, Movement, Employees salary, Ethics religion, Place living.
     Used in the Next 12 window, in the interpretations when you click on the earth element of the pillar and when you click on the name of the palace.

    Calculation of Luck - a variant of Luck pillar calculation:
    Traditional - the classic calculation of the pillars of Luck according to the Feng Shui theory;
    New - more accurate calculation according to the method of Degtyarev V.I. 

    Sunrise - what is the moment of sunrise to use in the calculations:
    Upper edge of disk - when the upper edge of the solar disk appears above the horizon;
    Center of disc - when the center of the sun disc appears above the horizon. 

    Begin of the day - when should the day start:
    Begin of the Rat - when the Rat's Chinese hour begins;
    Mid of the Rat - when the second half of the Chinese hour of the Rat begins. 

    Qi Phase Name - Various variations of the Qi phase names. for
    1st Var: womb, rear,  birth, bath, wearing hat, arriving officer, imperial prosperity, waning, sickness,
death, grave, extinct;
    2nd Var: conception, nurture, birth, bath, preparation, arrival, peak, aging, sickness, death, burial, end;
    3rd Var: Neutral Chi Phases, Neutral Chi Phases, Luminous Chi Phases, Dying Chi Phases, Luminous Chi Phases, Luminous Chi Phases, Luminous Chi Phases, Neutral Chi Phases, Neutral Chi Phases, Dying Chi Phases, Dying Chi Phases, Dying Qi phase. 
    The Default settings button is used to set the default application settings. 
    In addition, on this tab, you can switch the interface Language (Russian, English).
   

    On the CALCULATIONS tab, you can configure the application for the desired merging of elements option:
    Triple - whether it is necessary to take into account the merger of the triple elements (they will be displayed, but they will not participate in the calculations);
    Triple Merge Priority - when using the fusion of the triple elements in the calculations, which merging to do first. If the box is checked, the triple elements will merge first, then double merges, otherwise it will be the other way around;
    Accounting the change element of personality - as a result of the merging of the elements, the element of the personality can change. This checkbox determines whether such a situation should be taken into account in the calculations.
    In addition to the option of merging the elements, on this tab you can specify:
    Accounting the empty branches - whether it is necessary to take into account empty earthly branches in the calculations;
    Accounting the type "Follow the leader" - the calculated value of the favorableness of the elements will change if the personality type "Follow the leader" is determined;
    Accounting the type "Superstrong personality" - whether it is necessary to take into account the corresponding type of personality in the calculations. When using this type of personality, it is necessary to indicate the minimum strength of the personality, above which this type will be taken into account.  

    On the PILLARS tab, you can configure the degree of influence of each pillar on the calculation results (from 0 to 1):
    Pillar of conception with coefficient: - whether it is necessary to use the pillar of conception in the calculations. If this pillar will be used, then with what coefficient will it participate.
    Pillar of life with a coefficient: - whether it is necessary to use the pillar of life in the calculations. If this pillar is used, then with what coefficient will it participate.
    The rest of the pillars cannot be excluded from the calculations, you can only indicate the coefficient with which this pillar will participate in the calculations. If the value of the coefficient is equal to 0, the pillar will not affect the calculation results.
