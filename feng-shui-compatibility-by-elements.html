Feng Shui People Compatibility

Compatibility by elements

    Feng Shui experts can use the Compatibility by elements command and analyze the impact of the elements of the year and birthday on the relationship between partners.
    Everyone may be interested in the information presented in the form of graphs in the lower part of the window in the By the elements group. This information shows the mutual influence of one partner on another. First, the change in the general index of harmony for each partner is shown (for both partners, the harmony index increases by 71% and 4%). Further, the mutual influence on aspects of each personality (Personality, Intelligence, Wealth, Power, Resources) is shown, then the influence on the activities of each partner (Creative, Financial, Public, Scientific, Administrative, Independent). The influence on finances for both is quite good, but the first partner has a suppression of social activities, and the second - administrative.
