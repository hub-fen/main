Feng Shui Programs

      Feng Shui is a teaching on how to live in harmony with the surrounding reality. Feng Shui includes many methods and techniques based on certain laborious calculations based on the Chinese lunar calendar. To perform such calculations and present the results obtained in a form convenient for you, programs are intended, which will be discussed below.

      These programs will be useful for professionals, amateurs of Feng Shui, and everyone who is interested in cosmic rhythms and their influence on our lives.

Feng Shui Forecasts (Horoscopes) - a program for fans and those interested in Feng Shui. May be useful for professionals too

      This program will be a good assistant in planning various cases, based on the forecasts that it generates for you.

Based on several forecasting methods, a generalized short and detailed forecast for the current day, month and year is formed. Contains a calendar of good days of the month and an indicator of good hours in the day.

Feng Shui People Compatibility - for fans and those interested in Feng Shui. May be useful for professionals too

      This application will help determine the compatibility of the two people you are interested in and suggest how much they fit together, based on Feng Shui calculations. It will also indicate the strengths and weaknesses in their relationship.

In addition, the application allows you to make a different analysis of the relationship in a group of people, to determine potential conflicts between them, how well a person will be able to enter a particular group, etc.

Compatibility is determined by the elements and stars at birth. For each of these datasets, a detailed description and a summary score for compatibility is generated.
To obtain more accurate calculations, the application contains 136 609 settlements of the world with the possibility of specifying coordinates.

It will be a good assistant in assessing love (marriage) relationships, as well as in determining the compatibility of a group of people to achieve maximum results.
