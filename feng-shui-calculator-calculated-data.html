Feng Shui calculator

Calculated data

    To display the Calculated data window, you must execute the command of the same name, which is located in the left menu "≡".

    In this window, you can view and analyze a variety of information related to both the person's date of birth and the calculated date. Since there is a lot of information, it is divided into tabs that are located at the bottom of the window.

    On the FAVO... tab, strength, auspiciousness, excess and lack of elements for the person and for the calculated date are displayed.

    Such windows can be moved (they remember their position) by grabbing the title bar (blue line). This is convenient when using large-screen devices. If such a window is not closed (the "x" button), then the next time the application is launched, it will appear automatically.

    On the COND... tab, personality conductivity, personality transit conductivity and the estimated date conductivity are displayed.

    On the CONS... tab, numbers-stars, constellations and Jian Chu are displayed for personality and calculated date. 

    The CYCLES tab displays large cycles and Chinese dates for the date of birth and calculated date.

    The TIME tab displays the start of the Chinese two-hour clock for the date of birth and the calculated date, and the sunrises and sunsets for those dates. If the place of birth of a person is not specified, then information on the date of birth is not calculated and the cells in the first column will be empty. 

    The beginning and duration of the Chinese "two hours" will be different if you enable the use of variable hours, which depend on the geographical latitude of the place of birth and calculation (see Application Settings).

    If you turn on the Sunny time checkbox, all times on this tab will be represented by local solar time.
